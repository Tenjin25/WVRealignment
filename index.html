Research Findings: West Virginia Electoral Realignment

West Virginia Scope

Coverage: 1950-2024 across 55 counties.

Contests: President, U.S. Senate, Governor, Secretary of State, Attorney General, Auditor, Commissioner of Agriculture, and State Treasurer.

Pipeline: Clarity/OpenElections precinct-county inputs aggregated into WV county-level JSON.

Statewide Presidential Trend

2024: Republican +42.69 statewide.
